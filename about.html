Service
My faith plays a huge role in my life. Through my current church,
Jacob's Porch, I am involved in Bible study and worship planning.
Previously, at my home church, I served as youth representative on
the Church Board, and have been attending Leadership Lab since 2013.
Through my high school youth group, I went on yearly mission trips
to Kyle, South Dakota, and developed relationships with the Oglala
Lakota community there, who have been affected by systemic racism.
Because of this experience, spreading love and acceptance in opposition
to systemic racism is something that I am passionate about.
